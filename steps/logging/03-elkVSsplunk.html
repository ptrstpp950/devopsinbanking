Splunk VS ELK

Most popular log aggregators in onsite model are:
  • ELK - Elastic Search with Kibana
  • Splunk
  • Systems based on: HDFS and Kafka
Splunk - It's a very powerful, mainly on premises solution. More or less expensive :)
If you only want a "supergrep" command it is probably a wrong choice

ELK - License fees not incurred, but you'll end up spending $ on building various features, beacuse ELK doesn't have them. It is a "supergrep". Rest you will have to do by your own

About the rest - I don't know. I didn't used them
