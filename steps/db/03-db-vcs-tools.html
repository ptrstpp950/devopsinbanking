The tools that belong to the same class retain the same principles and ideas. If you are familiar with one of such tool, you will find it pretty easy to learn how to work with another one. Nevertheless, the functionality behind them might differ a lot, so it’s important to carefully choose one that fulfils your project’s needs the most. A small funny history from my life.
